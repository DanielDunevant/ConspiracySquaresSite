PRIVACY POLICY FOR CLEVERGEN LEARN

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application CleverGen Learn (“An educational app that helps its users learn mathematics as well as other subjects”) that was created by CleverGen for mobile devices.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download and register the Application with us. Registration with us is optional. However, please keep in mind that you may not be able to use some of the features offered by the Application unless you register with us. When you register with us and use the Application, you generally provide:

(a) your Google or Apple account information, which contains your name, email address, age, user name, password and other registration information

(b) transaction-related information, such as when you make purchases, respond to any offers, or download or use applications from us

(c) information you provide us when you contact us for help

(d) credit card information if you purchase a subscription to this Application for the removal of advertisements

(e) information you enter into our system by using the Application, such as contact information, information regarding your saved progress, and information regarding add-ons created for this app

We may also use the information you provided us to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices, and marketing promotions.

Automatically Collected Information

We work with analytics companies to help us understand how the Application is being used, such as the frequency and duration of usage. In doing so, we collect anonymous data from every user of this Application to monitor traffic and fix bugs. In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application, including the progress you make while learning the subjects in this Application and information about any add-ons you may have downloaded, created, or uploaded.

Application Add-On Privacy

With respect to any add-ons you may choose to upload to or download from CleverGen Learn, we take the privacy and confidentiality of such add-ons seriously. We encrypt all add-ons, and permanently delete any redacted edits you make to add-ons. If you choose to make an add-on public, we recommend you redact any and all references to companies, people, and addresses, as we can't protect public data and we are not responsible for any violation of privacy law you may be liable for.

Application Information Usage

We only use your personal information to provide you the CleverGen Learn services or to communicate with you about the services or the Application. We employ industry standard techniques to protect against unauthorized access of data about you that we store, including personal information. CleverGen Learn is operated from the United States. If you are using the app from outside the U.S., you agree to any processing of any personal information you provide us according to this policy. CleverGen may contact you, by email or other means. For example, CleverGen may send you promotional emails relating to CleverGen or other third parties CleverGen feels you would be interested in, or communicate with you about your use of the CleverGen Learn. CleverGen may also use technology to alert us via a confirmation email when you open an email from us. You can modify your email notification preferences by clicking the appropriate link included in the footer of email notifications. If you do not want to receive email from CleverGen, please opt out of receiving emails at the bottom of any CleverGen emails or by editing your profile preferences on our website.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

We work with advertisers and third party advertising networks, who need to know how you interact with advertising provided in the Application which helps us keep the cost of the Application low. Advertisers and advertising networks use some of the information collected by the Application, including, but not limited to, the unique identification ID of your mobile device and your mobile telephone number. These third parties may also obtain anonymous information about other applications you’ve downloaded to your mobile device, the mobile websites you visit, your non-precise location information (e.g., your zip code), and other non-precise location information in order to help analyze and serve anonymous targeted advertising on the Application and elsewhere. If you’d like to opt-out from third party use of this type of information to help serve targeted advertising, please visit the section entitled “Opt-out” below.

There are multiple opt-out options for users of this Application: You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network. You may at any time opt-out from further allowing us to have access to your location data by describing how users can manage their location preferences either at the app or device level.
